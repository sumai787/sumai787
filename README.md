# Hi there, I'm Sumaiya 👋

I'm an MSc Data Science graduate interested in how data can help people make better decisions, especially when problems are messy, technical, or have real-world consequences.

My work sits between **applied analytics, machine learning, business intelligence and responsible AI**. I have worked on projects involving healthcare machine learning, smart agriculture, university ranking analytics, and impact-driven microfinance. What connects these projects is the same question: how can data be used in a way that is useful, explainable and grounded in context?

At the moment, I’m shaping my next steps around **trustworthy AI, decision-support systems and knowledge retention**, while continuing to build practical experience in analytics, visualisation and applied research.

---

## 🌱 What I’m drawn to

- **Responsible AI:** not only whether a model works, but whether it can be explained, questioned and trusted.
- **Decision-support:** using data to make complex problems easier to understand.
- **Applied research:** connecting technical methods with people, context and real-world consequences.
- **Sustainability and public value:** exploring how data can support social, environmental and organisational impact.
- **Knowledge retention:** exploring how AI can help preserve, retrieve and question important knowledge in complex technical settings.

## ✨ Work I’m building from

- **Machine learning:** healthcare and credit-risk projects focused on evaluation, interpretation and responsible model use.
- **Smart agriculture:** satellite and weather data used to explore nitrogen-related decision support.
- **Business intelligence:** visual analytics work using education and organisational data.
- **Impact-driven finance:** MSc dissertation work on transparent microfinance decision support.
- **Trustworthy AI:** growing interest in knowledge retention and evidence-based systems for complex settings.
